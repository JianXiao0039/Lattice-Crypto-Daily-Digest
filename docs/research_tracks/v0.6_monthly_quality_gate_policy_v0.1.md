# v0.6 Monthly Quality Gate Policy v0.1

## Decision

`monthly_quality_gate_ready_with_limits`

## Purpose

The monthly quality gate is a deterministic, manual-only quality check for generated Monthly Lattice Paper Radar artifacts.

It is not a manual annotation workflow, human-gold metric workflow, classifier evaluation, ranking model, or paper inclusion system.

## Pass Criteria

A monthly radar output passes when:

- Monthly Markdown and JSON exist.
- Monthly JSON is parseable.
- Core Papers of the Month, Direction Trends, Reading Priority, Source Health Summary, and TODO_VERIFY sections exist.
- Top papers have recommendation rationales.
- Top-paper rationales are not keyword-only.
- Evidence basis is present.
- TODO_VERIFY is present.
- Reading action is clear.
- Title-only or metadata-only records remain conservative.
- Source-starved or degraded-source status is explicit.
- No user annotation fields are required.

## Result Values

- `pass`: quality score at least 80 and no blockers.
- `pass_with_limits`: quality score 65-79 or non-blocking warnings.
- `fail`: quality score below 65 or any blocker.

## Current Real Artifact Result

For `2026-06`, the quality gate returns `pass_with_limits` with score 79.

The gate is usable for v0.6 monthly quality stabilization, with known limits around bilingual rendering and reading-action alignment.
