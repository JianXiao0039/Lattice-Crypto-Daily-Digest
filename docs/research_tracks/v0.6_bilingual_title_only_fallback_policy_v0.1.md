# v0.6 Bilingual Title-Only Fallback Policy v0.1

Status: `title_only_fallback_ready`.

Title-only or metadata-only records must not receive invented method, construction, attack, benchmark, or contribution summaries.

Required behavior:

- confidence remains `title_only` or `metadata_supported`;
- Chinese work summary says evidence is limited to title or metadata;
- English work summary says method and contribution are not established;
- core novelty is marked insufficient evidence;
- recommendation action remains conservative;
- TODO_VERIFY is present.

This fallback is an explanation guard, not a ranking or inclusion change.
