# Post-Tag Evidence Requirements v0.1

Permanent reliability thresholds require:

1. at least seven actual post-tag Daily runs;
2. at least one post-tag Weekly synthesis and handoff;
3. normal-network observations;
4. degraded-network observations;
5. IACR direct-fetch and cache-hit observations;
6. at least one Semantic Scholar candidate-bearing observation;
7. green Ubuntu and Windows CI for the relevant release line;
8. no silent daily Markdown or JSON omission;
9. complete weekly coverage after authoritative backfill;
10. manual confirmation that reliability and relevance quality remain separate.

Confidence progression:

- `low`: baseline-only or incomplete sample;
- `provisional`: minimum evidence classes present;
- `mature`: repeated windows show stable behavior and thresholds have been reviewed.
