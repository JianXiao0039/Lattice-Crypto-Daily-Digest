Minimal monthly design:

1. Read existing daily and weekly JSON artifacts for a calendar month.
2. Preserve source-health caveats and missing-day coverage.
3. Summarize stable research themes without changing ranking or taxonomy.
4. Include rationale summaries only when daily/weekly rationale fields exist.
5. Emit manual-only Markdown/JSON under a future explicitly reviewed path.

Monthly sections:

- Month coverage and source-health reliability.
- Top lattice/PQC research themes.
- Reading queue candidates by 精读 / 扫读 / 暂存.
- Track handoff summary.
- TODO_VERIFY for source-starved days and metadata-only papers.
