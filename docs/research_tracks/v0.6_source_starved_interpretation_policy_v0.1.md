# v0.6 Source-Starved Interpretation Policy v0.1

Status: `source_starved_interpretation_ready`.

Source-starved means source failures materially limited retrieval evidence.

Required interpretation:

- do not treat source-starved output as proof that no relevant papers exist;
- show degraded source-health status in Daily/Weekly/Monthly artifacts;
- include TODO_VERIFY for missing source coverage;
- recommend manual low-load retry later when compliant;
- distinguish source failure from quiet research activity.

Source-starved reporting is part of the public paper radar reliability layer and does not change ranking or paper inclusion.
