# v0.5 Track Evaluation Protocol v0.1

## Precision Sample

- sample records assigned to each track;
- include high-score, low-score, and TODO_VERIFY cases;
- preserve source and date traceability.

## False-Positive Review

- verify explicit lattice/PQC anchors;
- reject generic hash, commitment, privacy, AI, registration, or signature papers without anchors;
- record the exact cause of incorrect inclusion.

## False-Negative Review

- sample excluded/noise and background records;
- inspect known relevant titles from authoritative daily artifacts;
- record missed synonyms and whether a code change is justified.

## Explanation Quality

Every assignment should state:

- matched primitive or assumption;
- evidence text or metadata field;
- why the track is useful;
- uncertainty and TODO_VERIFY;
- non-claims.

## Handoff Usefulness

Rate whether the item helps related work, construction, proof, parameterization, implementation, comparison, or long-term watchlists.

## Change Gate

Do not change production ranking, taxonomy, or queries until the labeled sample demonstrates a specific precision/recall problem and the proposed change has regression tests.
