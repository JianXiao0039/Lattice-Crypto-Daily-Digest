# v0.6 Monthly Evidence Grounding Policy v0.1

## Decision

Evidence grounding check is ready.

## Policy

Monthly rationale must distinguish:

- abstract-derived evidence;
- conclusion-derived evidence, only when conclusion text exists;
- repository-note-derived evidence;
- metadata-derived evidence;
- title-only evidence.

If conclusion text is absent, the rationale must not claim that the conclusion says, shows, or proves anything.

If a paper is title-only or metadata-only, the rationale must be conservative and must include TODO_VERIFY.

For `2026-06`:

- missing evidence basis findings: none;
- missing TODO_VERIFY findings: none;
- title-only overclaim findings: none;
- hallucinated conclusion claims: none.
