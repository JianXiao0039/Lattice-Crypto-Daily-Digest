2. Should Skim

Policy:

- Use existing `priority_label`, `reading_priority_score`, `reading_priority`, relevance label, and relevance score.

Purpose:

The monthly view helps the user decide what to 精读, 扫读, 暂存, or ignore after seeing a month of Daily/Weekly radar evidence.
