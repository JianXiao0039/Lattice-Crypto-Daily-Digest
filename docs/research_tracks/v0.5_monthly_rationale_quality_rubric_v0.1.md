# v0.5 Monthly Rationale Quality Rubric v0.1

## Scope

This rubric evaluates real Monthly radar recommendation rationale. It does not change ranking, source fetching, taxonomy, query expansion, or paper inclusion.

## Score

- 0: missing or keyword-only.
- 1: vague and mostly keyword-based.
- 2: partially useful but missing problem, method, or contribution.
- 3: useful, evidence basis present, some `TODO_VERIFY`.
- 4: strong, grounded, actionable.
- 5: excellent, clearly helps decide reading priority and has high-quality or bilingual top-paper rationale.

## Required Checks

- Problem summary.
- Method, construction, attack, implementation, or benchmark summary.
- Contribution or core novelty where evidence supports it.
- Evidence basis.
- Radar relevance.
- Reading action.
- `TODO_VERIFY`.
- Overclaim risk.
- Keyword-only risk.

## Evidence Status Values

- `conclusion_supported`
- `abstract_supported`
- `metadata_supported`
- `title_only`
- `insufficient_evidence`

## Reading Action Quality

- `correct`
- `too_strong`
- `too_weak`
- `unclear`
- `unsupported`
