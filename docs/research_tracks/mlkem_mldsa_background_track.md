# MLWE / ML-KEM / ML-DSA Background Track

Status: public research track document.

## Purpose

Maintain background assets for standardized lattice/PQC schemes and implementation maturity without letting them overwhelm the short-term Module-SIS chameleon hash track.

## In-Scope

- ML-KEM / Kyber security and implementation.
- ML-DSA / Dilithium implementation and rejection sampling.
- FIPS 203 / FIPS 204 references.
- PQC implementation reproducibility.
- side-channel / fault / constant-time issues for lattice schemes.
- parameter estimation and security review.
- MLWE / RLWE background relevant to standardized schemes.

## When to Keep Watching

Keep papers that:

- affect implementation security;
- clarify standard behavior;
- provide reproducible parameter or audit checklists;
- connect to MLWE / ML-KEM / ML-DSA assumptions;
- help maturity of PQC research vocabulary.

## When to Escalate

Escalate to reading if:

- the paper directly concerns ML-KEM / ML-DSA security;
- it offers reproducible checks;
- it may influence a public implementation-security artifact;
- it helps understand parameterization for lattice primitives.

## When Not to Generate Artifact

Do not generate an artifact if:

- the paper is only background;
- original paper is not read;
- the claim is too strong and unverified;
- it does not support current track priorities;
- the work is generic PQC without lattice scheme details.
