# v0.5 AI4Lattice Longline Scope v0.1

## Include

- sparse LWE/RLWE/MLWE attack support;
- coordinate ranking and selection for hybrid attacks;
- learning-guided BKZ, primal, dual, hybrid, sieving, or pruning interfaces;
- Swin-guided structured-coordinate selection;
- attack cost prediction and reproducible toy baselines;
- negative-cyclic RLWE/MLWE modeling when technically explicit.

## Required Baselines

- classical ranking or selection method;
- BKZ/primal/dual/hybrid interface;
- ablation against non-learning methods;
- realistic scope that avoids end-to-end real-parameter key-recovery claims.

## Exclude

- generic AI, LLM, optimization, or federated-learning work without lattice-cryptanalysis anchors;
- purely predictive demos with no cryptanalytic objective or baseline.
